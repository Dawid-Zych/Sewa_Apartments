About us

Located just a 20-minute walk from Krakow Central Train Station, Apartamenty Sewa provides a convenient base when visiting Krakow. The property is located in a popular entertainment district, just a 5-minute walk from the Cloth Hall, Kraków's Main Market Square and the Historical Museum of the City of Kraków.

Additional amenities at the property include parking and a tour desk. In addition, the property's multilingual staff will be happy to advise you on what to see during your stay.

Apartamenty Sewa is well-located for access to Krakow's main tourist attractions, with the Academy of Music in Krakow within a 10-minute drive. Guests of the property can visit places such as the National Museum in Krakow and Wawel Royal Castle.
